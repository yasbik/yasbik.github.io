Who We Are

A Legacy of
Trust & Precision

For over 32 years, Tista Surveyors BD has been a trusted name in insurance loss adjusting and valuation. Rooted in integrity and professionalism, we help clients recover from losses by delivering accurate surveys and fair assessments.

Starting in Dhaka in 1991, we have expanded to Khulna and Chattogram, offering services across six licensed areas: Fire, Motor, Marine, Hull, Engineering Breakdown, and Others.

Enlisted with 14 major banks, serving domestic and international clients, and contributing to government projects. Led by K.N.M Khorshed Alam, founding member and current President of BISA.
