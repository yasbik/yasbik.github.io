About Us

For over 32 years, Tista Surveyors BD has been a trusted name in insurance loss adjusting and valuation. Rooted in integrity and professionalism, we help clients recover from losses by delivering accurate surveys and fair assessments.

Starting in Dhaka in 1991, we have expanded to Khulna and Chattogram, offering services across six licensed areas: Fire, Motor, Marine, Hull, Engineering Breakdown, and Others. Our team of expert engineers and surveyors bring innovation, precision, and commitment to every case.

We are proud to be enlisted with 14 major banks, work with both domestic and international clients, and contribute to several government projects. Through continuous training — including programs with BIA and international partners — we maintain a skilled, future-ready workforce.

Led by K.N.M Khorshed Alam, a founding member and current President of BISA, we continue to uphold industry standards and set new benchmarks in survey excellence.

Our Services

  • Loss Adjustment

    We investigate insurance claims to determine the extent of damage and recommend fair compensation, ensuring transparency and accuracy for both insurers and clients.

  • Asset Valuation

    Our expert valuation services provide precise assessments of property, assets, and equipment to help clients understand true market value for insurance, sale, or audit purposes.

  • Pre-Insurance Risk Inspection

    We conduct thorough risk assessments before insurance coverage begins, helping insurers and clients identify vulnerabilities and ensure informed policy decisions.

  • Post Landing / Pre-Shipment Survey

    From verifying cargo conditions after arrival to inspections before export, we ensure goods are properly documented, minimizing risks and disputes in international trade.
